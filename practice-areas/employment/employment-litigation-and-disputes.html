Employment Litigation and Disputes

Aggressive defense of employment claims and disputes. Our experienced litigators protect your business interests in complex employment matters throughout Texas.

Overview

When employment disputes escalate to litigation, you need experienced trial attorneys who understand both the legal complexities and the business implications. Our employment litigators have successfully defended employers in a wide range of employment disputes, from individual claims to class action lawsuits.
